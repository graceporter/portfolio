Biography

About Me:

Hi! My name is Grace Porter and I am a first year student in the Interactive Design Program at SAIT. My major is in User Experience design. This is my 4th time changing programs, and I previously earned a diploma in Environmental Reclamation from Medicine Hat College. I was born and raised in Medicine Hat, and this is my first year living in Calgary. Before coming to SAIT, I worked at a Nature Centre in Police Point Park, in Medicine Hat. I learned a lot about the local flora and fauna, as well as event planning, leading school groups, and organizing programs!
